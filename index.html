About Emma_GIS

EMMA_GIS Geospatial Services is a Nigerian-based geospatial consulting firm providing data-driven location intelligence and spatial solutions that support smarter decision-making for governments, institutions, and organizations. We specialize in transforming complex spatial data into clear maps, interactive dashboards, and actionable insights that help decision-makers understand problems, plan effectively, and deliver sustainable outcomes. Our work sits at the intersection of GIS, remote sensing, data visualization, and emerging AI-powered spatial analysis. Founded to bridge the gap between data and impact, EMMA_GIS supports projects in urban planning, housing and land administration, infrastructure development, environmental management, agriculture, and disaster risk management.

What We Do At EMMA_GIS, we deliver end-to-end geospatial solutions, including: • Spatial data collection, validation, and analysis • Thematic and policy-oriented mapping • Interactive web maps, dashboards, and story maps • GIS support for housing, land governance, and infrastructure projects • Custom geospatial applications and decision-support tools Our solutions are designed to be practical, visual, and decision-ready, ensuring our clients can move from insight to action with confidence.

Our Impact We have contributed to and executed impactful geospatial projects across Nigeria, including: • Accident Hotspot Mapping in AMAC, FCT, integrating land-use, infrastructure, and topology datasets to improve urban safety and planning. • GIS-Enabled State Mapping for the Federal Ministry of Housing and Urban Development, now used within the Ministry to support data-driven housing and land policy formulation. • Environmental and infrastructure mapping initiatives, supporting development monitoring, resilience planning, and spatial transparency.

Capacity Building & Training Beyond consultancy, EMMA_GIS is committed to building geospatial capacity. We provide practical GIS training and professional development programs for individuals, institutions, and government agencies, focusing on real-world applications, institutional workflows, and policy-relevant outcomes.

Leadership EMMA_GIS is led by Emmanuel Okafor, a GIS Specialist and ESRI community member with hands-on experience supporting government programs, housing initiatives, and spatial innovation projects across Nigeria.

Our Philosophy We believe every location tells a story and when powered by accurate data and smart visualization, that story can drive transparency, efficiency, and sustainable development.
